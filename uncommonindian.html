DAY 1

PAUL D'SOUZA :-



Mr. Paul D'souza A self motivated innovator he got his forst breakthrough while working in an agro firm, form then onwards he focused his attention to his field of passion Mechanical Horology and started designing horological devices. This 38 year old inventor has a mechanical perpetual calendar, three large Date Display mechanisms and a differential escape mechanism as inventions and has 5 granted patents in the field of mechanical horology. A TEDx speaker, he views to extend his inventions to all possible fields and places.

REVATHI ROY:-



Mrs. Revathi Roy, an enthusiastic entrepreneur, is a doctorate in Economics, alumni of St Xaviers College Mumbai and a post graduate from Mumbai University. She worked as Management trainee in Benett, Coleman and Co., Ltd, India Today, Current Weekly and family business. She has a great passion for driving, and converted the same to a business by starting the first ever Taxi Service driven by women for women only in 2007. Co founder of VIIRA CABS. A Taxi service exclusively for women by women.

SANDEEP BARUAH:-



Mr. Sandeep Bauruah, A HAM Radio reasearcher from Assam, India who got his radio license way back in 1989 when he was just 19 years old. Holding a Masters degree in agriculture, he is also an gazetted officer in Assam. Currently working on Promotional activities related to alternative communication systems, as second line of communication and dissemination of amateur radio information and technology to the school children, technical students through outreach programs like lecture and demonstration programs, workshops, training programs, print media Internet etc. Mr. Sandeep is a Operational in-charge of Vigyan Prasar Amateur Radio club Station VU2NCT and Delhi VHF Repeater Station VU2DLR.

Sandeep sir would give presentation on practical demonstration of ham radio technologies. The demonstration included APRS(R) http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Automatic_Packet_Reporting_System [Registered Trademark of Bob Bruninga, WB4APR, US Naval Academy] and SSTV http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slow-scan_television ham radio image transmission too. A smartphone was used to demonstrate how ham radio SSTV images can be received through ham radio on a smartphone "when cellphone networks may not work" during a disaster.

ANJANA KARUMATHIL:-



Mrs. Anjana Karumathil, an engineering graduate from NIT, Calicut, and Masters of Business Administration degree holder with distinction from Strathclyde Business School, Glasgow. She is a multi-faceted entrepreneur who specializes in corporate learning and development .Over 12 years, Mrs. Anjana has worked in increasingly responsible roles across IT, corporate strategy and organization development. She has been a member of the think tank of Deloitte's Chief Strategy Officer and Chief-of-Staff for the Chief Learning Officer. She also worked as a senior project manager at Tata Consultancy Services Limited advising clients on technology solutions and travelling around the world to implement multimillion dollar software projects. She has passion for Carnatic music, travelling and photography.
