Makers Fete '14



Fep-Si's Makers Fete is a platform for all the change makers of the country to showcase and display change making ideas, creativity, inventions, resourcefulness and start-ups that in any way would help in making the world a better place to live in. Makers Fete is a gathering of tech enthusiasts, crafters, tinkerers, hobbyists, engineers, science clubs, students, and commercial exhibitors. All of these “Makers” come together to showcase their ideas and to share what they have learned. It aims to keep things simple; very simple. Anyone with an idea that can bring any minute change in the society, the way of life, comforts, making the world a better place to live in, is welcome to be a part of the innovative and yet prestigious 'Maker Family'. It emphasizes to bring together a community of makers and give them a platform to showcase and exhibit their ideas, products and creations.



Why Makers Fete?


The world is full of change makers and curiosity that drives them, so fueling their drive we gather all creators, makers, tinkerers and hobbyists to celebrate the festival of making because 'the world is what we make of it'. Different s of Makers fete like Maker Workshops, Maker Talks, Manifestations, The Uncommon Indian etc, provide all makers a big hub to gather, share the knowledge , grow individually and in turn lead to the growth of the nation. We, the team of Makers Fete, hope all the participants to do the same by sharing what they learn here, thereby helping in potential growth of our nation. Planning to revive the old attitude "Sharing for Development", we at Makers Fete celebrate innovations and ideas.

About VIT University



We are happy to introduce VIT University to you; a cosmopolitan university enriched with diverse cultures with an in-house strength of over 25,000 students representing every state of India and 52 countries around the globe.

The campus accounts for an infrastructure of international standards. The campus is spread across an area of 400 acres with state of art infrastructure facilities including a mega outdoor stadium, many auditoriums, conference halls, smart class rooms, galleries, outdoor grounds, outdoor meeting and gathering venues, state- of-the-art laboratories, digital library, twenty three hostels, gyms, swimming pools , auditoria etc. on way to fulfill the vision of the founder and Chancellor Dr. G. Viswanathan to make it truly world class.

On a whole, it offers an unparalleled venue for a never before known Maker Experience. More information about the University can be found here.

About Fep-Si



Forum for Empowering People through Social Innovation & entertainment (Fep-Si) is a national level NGO which works on different social issues by forming various student groups and implementing activities that benefit the society. In addition to this, it aims to build up a potential platform, where it offers the scope for an ‘individual’ to develop and to render his contribution for a better tomorrow.

Fep-Si is the birth child of likeminded Young Change Makers from VIT University with zeal and passion to do something for a better India. Instead of conventional approaches to tackle social issues, Fep-Si follows innovative way of fighting social evils and poverty through creativity, innovation and entertainment.

More details on Fep-Si’s projects, activities and achievements can be found here.
